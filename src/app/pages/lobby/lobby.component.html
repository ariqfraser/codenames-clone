Setup Instructions

Get at least 3 other humans. (min requement is 4 humans for a normal game, 2 on each team)

Enter player names in the "Red Team" and "Blue Team" boxes. Alternatively enter a team name in each box.

If you entered individual player names. You can press "shuffle teams" to shuffle teams. (optional).

Select the quantity of bombs you would like in the game. The higher number the harder. (optional).

Select what team should start first (optional).

Press play.
